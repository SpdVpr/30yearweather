## ðŸ”¥ Pro Tips

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Most important:** City name + "Best Time to Visit" (largest text)
- **Secondary:** Data points (medium size, icons help)
- **Tertiary:** Branding (small but visible)

### Color Psychology
- **Orange (#ea580c):** Energy, adventure, warmth (perfect for travel)
- **Blue (#0ea5e9):** Trust, reliability, data-driven
- **White:** Clean, professional, easy to read

### Pinterest Best Practices
1. **Text overlays:** Always add text to images (pins with text get 25% more engagement)
2. **Vertical format:** 2:3 ratio performs best (that's why 1000x1500)
3. **Clear branding:** URL visible but not overwhelming
4. **Actionable content:** "Plan your trip" > "Weather data"
